What is this about

What's Behind
the Label?

Many products sold to Canadian consumers are connected to supply chains where workers face unsafe conditions, forced labour, or child exploitation. These practices remain hidden due to complex global systems and limited transparency.

Behind The Label exists to change that. We expose what corporations don't want you to see — and give you the tools to act on it.

The Problem

Why This Matters

Existing systems are broken. Consumers are left in the dark. We built something to fix that.

01 —

Hidden Harm

Consumers often unknowingly support exploitative labour practices embedded deep in global supply chains — invisible to the naked eye.

02 —

Limited Accountability

Existing laws rely heavily on self-reporting and weak enforcement mechanisms that allow corporations to operate without consequence.

03 —

Power of Awareness

Public pressure and informed consumer choices drive ethical change faster — and more durably — than policy changes alone.
