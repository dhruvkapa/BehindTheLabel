The Programme

How Rewards Will Work

🔍

01 —

Use the Tool

Install our browser extension and scan brands while you shop online. Our extension flags supply chain risks and highlights ethical alternatives in real time.

✅

02 —

Choose Ethical Brands

When you choose a brand with a verified clean supply chain, our extension recognises your decision and unlocks exclusive discount codes from our partner network.

🎁

03 —

Redeem Your Reward

Apply your discount at checkout. Every purchase through a partner brand helps fund our research, keeps our data up to date, and directly supports supply chain transparency.

Partnership Opportunity

Partner
With Purpose

Accepting Applications — 2026

We're actively seeking ethical brands, certified fair-trade retailers, and B-Corp companies who want to reach an audience that genuinely cares about where products come from.

In exchange for offering exclusive discounts to our community, partner brands receive verified placement in our extension, a trust badge on our platform, and direct exposure to conscious consumers across Canada and beyond.

All partners are independently vetted against ILO standards, the Walk Free Foundation's Global Slavery Index, and the US DOL's TVPRA list before listing.
