What’s Really Behind the Label

Forced labour and child exploitation exist in global supply chains — often hidden behind everyday products.

What’s Behind the Label?

Many products sold to Canadian consumers are connected to supply chains where workers face unsafe conditions, forced labour, or child exploitation. These practices remain hidden due to complex global systems and limited transparency.

Why This Matters

Hidden Harm

Consumers often unknowingly support exploitative labour practices.

Limited Accountability

Existing laws rely heavily on self-reporting and weak enforcement.

Power of Awareness

Public pressure drives ethical change faster than policy alone.

Our Solution

We’re building a browser extension that alerts users when products may be linked to unethical labour practices, using trusted third-party sources.

  • ✔ Real-time alerts while shopping online
  • ✔ Clear explanations with cited sources
  • ✔ Designed for awareness, not shaming
